Pizza on the Grill

No pizza oven? No problem! Make your homemade pizza on the grill for that classic smoky, wood fired oven flavor. And even if you have a pizza oven, you might prefer the grill from time to time.

  • Pizza Dough
  • Garlic Oil
  • Quarter Cup Tomato Sauce
  • Quarter Cup Sliced Olives
  • 2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella
Steps:
  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Mix in the floor, 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  4. Turn onto a lightly floured surface. Knead until smooth, about 8 minutes.
  5. Set aside to rise until doubled, about 1 hour.
  6. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
  7. Add toppings on the dough.
  8. Place in the oven and cook until the cheese melts.
